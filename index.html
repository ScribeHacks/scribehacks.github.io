Write the Code of the Future

We allow those in middle school to join us, but with added parental permission. If you are in college you are welcome to mentor or give speeches at the hackathon. If you want to be a mentor, you can sign up Here or if you want to sponsor you can email us at sponsors@scribehacks.tech

At ScribeHacks we want people to make something that will help the future instead of the present or past. While this is open ended, we believe that it will spark creativity in each person to think about the future and write the code for it. We believe that by giving a theme that makes you think about the future, you will better plan and develop for it. Whether you want to make an app that will track the energy being used to a robot that can help people who are suffering, we welcome all ideas and hacks that want to make the future of our world a better place.

ScribeHacks is absolutely free! There is no entry fee, ScribeHacks will provide you with plenty of food, snacks, swag, and prizes throughout the 24 hours. We can provide all of this due to our wonderful sponsors!

Please bring a valid form of identification like a student id or driver's license, a computer (preferably a laptop), chargers, and any hardware you will use for your hack. You can also bring a sleeping bag, pillow, toiletries, and a couple changes of clothes, food will be provided at the event. No firearms, weapons, alcohol, or illegal drugs are allowed on campus.
